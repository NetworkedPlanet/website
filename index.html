Who we are

We offer consultancy in anything data related and training so that your organisation can tackle digital information management challenges with confidence. We also build clever software and tools that are built to conform to official standards. We won't push products at you that don't fit your requirements.

At Networked Planet we're committed to using our years of experience with information management to help companies that are helping others. Our "data for good" projects focus on helping charities and voluntary organisations reap the same benefits to data control that the private sector have had access to for years.

Every project should be built from the ground up, tailor made to your needs. To start that conversation just drop us a line.
