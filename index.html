Who we are

At Networked Planet we're committed to using our years of experience with information management to help companies that are helping others. Our "data for good" projects focus on helping charities and voluntary organisations be more effective in their use of data.

We offer consultancy and training to help organisations tackle digital information management challenges with confidence. We also build standards-based open source software and tools.

Every project should be built from the ground up, tailor-made to your needs. To start that conversation just drop us a line.
